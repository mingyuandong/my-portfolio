    Mingyuan Dong is an award-winning industrial designer, artist, and author. Her design practice integrates human-computer interaction with tangible devices, aiming to foster seamless behavioral change, enhance mental well-being, and promote inclusivity for people of all abilities. She has received numerous art and design awards, including Gold in the 18th International Design Awards (IDA). She is the author of novel Not Sci-Fi, which explores themes of design and eco-surrealism. With a triple-major, interdisciplinary background from UC Berkeley and a Master's degree in Industrial Design from the Rhode Island School of Design (RISD), she aspires to create technologies that augment the experience of humanity across diverse worlds — literally and metaphorically.
